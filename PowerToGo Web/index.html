Trend of Power bank sharing business
Market
In 2023, the European power bank sharing market size was USD 3.67 billion. In 2028, the researchers forecast the volume of this market to be USD 10.6 billion, with an annual growth rate of 23.6% during this period. Power bank sharing is also the part of the sharing economy that has shown stable growth in recent years.
Key benefits
Demand in the power bank sharing market is high and stable because people need to charge their devices all the time and this need doesn't change with the seasons. A quick return on investment makes the business more attractive. Power bank station can recoup its investment in a couple of months.
